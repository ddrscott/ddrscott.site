### Tips for Your Poster Board Presentation

1. **Clear and Bold Text:**
   - Use large, bold fonts so the questions are easily readable from a distance.
   - Ensure high contrast between the text and the background for maximum legibility.

2. **Consistent Design:**
   - Keep a consistent design for all boards to maintain visual coherence.
   - Use a simple color scheme that aligns with the somber mood of the background music.

3. **Pacing:**
   - Practice the timing of your flips to match the tempo and mood of the music.
   - Allow enough time for viewers to read and reflect on each question before moving to the next.

4. **Engagement:**
   - Consider adding subtle visual elements or illustrations that complement the questions without overwhelming them.
   - Ensure your movements are smooth and deliberate to maintain the contemplative atmosphere.

5. **Background and Setting:**
   - Choose a clean, uncluttered background to keep the focus on the poster boards.
   - Ensure good lighting so the text is clearly visible.

6. **Music Choice:**
   - Select music that enhances the reflective mood without being too distracting.
   - Consider instrumental tracks that evoke the right emotional tone.
